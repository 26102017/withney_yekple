My ambitions

Currently in my first year at the Web School Factory, among the 3 majors offered to us - web design, web marketing and web development - I would like to specialize in the field of design in the coming year. Within this school, my goal is to push my abilities to the limit and to refine my expertise and skills in this field. In addition, I would like to train myself in web development independently in order to be more versatile, which will be useful for my professional project.

In life, I have always been a persevering person. I came to this school at the beginning of the year with no digital and software skills, yet that hasn't stopped me from being one of the best in my class. Dedicated in everything I undertake, I am convinced that I can go through with what I would like to achieve, no matter how much effort it will cost me, I am not afraid of danger and the unknown, that's why I am here!

Gaining experiences

First of all I would like to work in a company as a webdesigner in order to acquire the skills I will need to start as a freelancer. On the other hand, I would do some web development services thanks to what I would have learned at the Web School Factory and from myself during my training years.

Skills improvement

Then, I would take a year (or more), to train myself in depth in web development and concretize my professional project that I would have started during my years at the Web School Factory and next to my work.

Project achievement

Finally, once I consider that I will have the necessary skills to realize my project, I will fully embark on its development and start my own business.
